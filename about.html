Kosai exists to unleash the potential of open source software - by giving open source maintainers the opportunity to live off their work, and software developers the opportunity to rely on its security.​


SOSSHub is a platform that:​


1.    Helps open source maintainers get paid fairly for their work.​

2.   Helps software developers get patched open source code.​


Kosai is not an alert-generating SCA tool that creates work, nor are we a faux-pen source company looking to hoard profits made off the work of others.​


SOSSHub is the most efficient way to secure your open source dependencies within your existing development cadence. We bring the enterprise-grade support and commercial terms you expect from your proprietary software vendors to your open source footprint.​


And you can be assured that your investment in Kosai gets re-invested into the open source community you depend on.​
